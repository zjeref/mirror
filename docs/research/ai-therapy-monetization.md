What Converts Free Users to Paid\n\n1. **Personalization wall** -- Free gives generic; paid gives tailored recommendations, personalized insights, and AI that \"remembers\" you. Replika's memory feature is premium-only.\n2. **Progress tracking / data depth** -- Free shows basic mood; paid unlocks pattern analysis, longitudinal trends, and PDF reports for therapists.\n3. **Content depth** -- Free gives 5-10 exercises; paid unlocks the full library (Headspace, Calm, Wysa model).\n4. **Human escalation** -- AI is free; human coaching/therapy is paid (Wysa, Talkspace model).\n5. **Longer free trials convert better** -- 17-32 day trials achieve ~48.8% conversion vs. ~37% for shorter trials. Seven-day trials are industry standard but suboptimal.
